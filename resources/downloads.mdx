# Downloads

All Asimov v0 design files, simulation models, and meshes are available for download. The project is released under the CERN-OHL-S-2.0 license.

### Mechanical Design Files

The complete mechanical design is available in the `/mechanical` directory:

- **ASV0_lowerBody.STEP** - Complete lower body assembly in STEP format
- **naming_convention.png** - Joint and component naming reference
- **ankle_mechanism.md** - Detailed RSU (Revolute Spherical Universal) ankle mechanism documentation

<Note>
  The mechanical design is compatible with MJF (Multi Jet Fusion) 3D printing for low-volume manufacturing.
</Note>

## Motor Specifications

Motors are sourced from [Encos](http://encos.cn):

| Joint Type | Model | Application |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| Hip Pitch | EC-A6416-P2-25 | Hip flexion/extension (120 Nm) |
| Hip Roll | EC-A5013-H17-100 | Hip abduction/adduction (90 Nm) |
| Hip Yaw | EC-A3814-H14-107 | Hip rotation (60 Nm) |
| Knee Pitch | EC-A4315-P2-36 | Knee flexion/extension (75 Nm) |
| Ankle A | EC-A4310-P2-36 | Ankle dorsi/plantarflexion (36 Nm) |
| Ankle B | EC-A4310-P2-36 | Ankle inversion/eversion (36 Nm) |

<Note>
  Motor specifications and sourcing information are subject to availability. Contact the community for alternative sourcing options.
</Note>
